Importance of Database Security

Published: May 2025

In today's digital world, database security is one of the most crucial aspects of enterprise data management...

Key Elements of Database Security

  • Access Control: Only authorized users can access data.
  • Encryption: Data is encrypted both at rest and in transit.
  • Auditing: Monitoring who accessed what and when.

Conclusion

Security must be at the heart of every database design and deployment strategy to ensure business continuity and trust.
